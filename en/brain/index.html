Brain-Prosthetic Interfaces

Read more about the projects of brain-prosthesis interfaces in which we have been working and that have fascinated researchers all over the world. We are always willing to develop novel technology solutions for the benefit of society with great sense of ethical responsibility.

Description


Brain-machine interfaces is a neuroengineering field that has had a significant growth over the last decade, due to the fact that such technique could be applied in a wide variety of fields, ranging from entertainment (videogames), to biomedical devices such as prosthetics and orthotics.

HSI Medical Solutions focuses on the development of non-invasive brain-prosthesis interfaces. We are encouraged to develop prostheses that are controlled through the non-invasive register of each patient’s brain activity.

How does it work?

The voltage fluctuations resulting from the electrical potential generated by millions of neurons can be measured along the scalp using a recording technique known as electroencephalography (EEG). The EEG signal is processed using time, frequency, or multiresolution analysis and its features are classified by a series of intelligent inference systems in order to determine the subjects' will to move their hands, to close their eyes, to sleep, etc.
