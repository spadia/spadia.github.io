Liderazgo

Comenzamos esta compañía cuando nos dimos cuenta que podíamos utilizar nuestras habilidades y conocimientos para desarrollar soluciones tecnológicas de alta calidad y bajo costo, con la finalidad de satisfacer las necesidades reales de la sociedad.

Eduardo López Arce Vivas

Biomedical Engineer specializing in electronics and PCB design, as well as in embedded applications specifically in signal processing and pattern recognition. Recognized worldwide in these engineering areas obtaining so far two refereed IEEE publications and one Springer-Verlag monograph chapter in revision, the three of them as first author. The Best Paper Award in the area of Human Machine Interaction at the 2013 IEEE International Conference on Human System Interaction due to his research in Brain-Machine Interfaces is one of his many recognitions.

Eduardo graduated in Biomedical Engineering from Instituto Tecnológico y de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey. He got the highest GPA of his bachelor's degree, obtaining the Honorable Mention of Excellence. He has worked in research laboratories in Germany, Spain, and Mexico, developing technological solutions specifically in the fields of electronics and informatics.

Eduardo Prado Ruiz

Eduardo is a Biomedical Engineer graduated from Instituto Tecnológico y de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey. He has been interested in open source solutions and coding since an early age. Reason that took him into the Entrepreneur Ecosystem. He started as an independent developer for iOS applications and Web newsletters and progress throught hardware programming, developing solutions for the Health system. Founded Radiatta productions at age 17 for music and media production, on 2013 he won Startup Weekend 2013 in Guadalajara, Mexico with TeRento.mx a 2nd hand book market for students also the same year he Co-Founded GreenBox a company focused on creating high-end Acoustic Amplifiers for mobile devices.

Currently he volunteers with Mexican RedCross on the implementation of a Biomedical Engineering Department and developing low cost blood pressure Sphygmomanometers.
